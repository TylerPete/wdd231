401(k) (Traditional)

Contribution Tax Treatment: Traditional (Pre-tax)

Tax-Deductible Contributions: Yes

Employment-Based: Yes

Employer Match: Common

Annual Contribution Limits (2025):
  • $23,500 employee deferral
  • $7,500 catch-up if 50+
  • Total $69,000 limit (employer + employee)
Withdrawal Tax/Restrictions:
  • Taxed as ordinary income
  • RMDs starting at 73
  • Loans and early withdrawals allowed but may have penalties
Other information:
  • One of the most common employer-sponsored retirement plans in the private sector
  • Employer matches are common and increase savings potential
  • Loans and hardship withdrawals may be permitted depending on plan rules
